About Amrita Amaravati campus

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati, is a premier NAAC A++ accredited, technology-driven campus near Mangalagiri, Andhra Pradesh, focusing on engineering, management, and sciences. Established on a 100-200 acre site, it offers modern infrastructure, high-value placements (up to ₹80.4 LPA in 2025), and industry-oriented research.
